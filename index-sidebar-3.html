About Me

My Name is Jeremiah Duku

Dedicated and community-minded student with a foundation in economics and mathematical concepts who is committed to making a difference in the world. During my gap year after high school, I worked with Feed the Hungry to help distribute resources to individuals with disabilities and others in need in my community, which helped me get a deeper grasp of economics. My understanding of empathy, community connection, and purposeful sustainability has grown because of this experience; I have also shown these values by participating in recycling projects with the Aselem Foundation. As I get ready for college, I want to use my knowledge of mathematics and economics to encourage critical thinking and problem-solving in my neighborhood. I can't wait to contribute to a learning environment that upholds these principles and fosters both intellectual and personal development.
